Overview

You can use the pricezilla web app to compare prices of home appliances by seraching and comparing prices from diffrent stores and ordering directly from the store. Her’s a quick rundown:

If you want to compare prices

  • Go to Pricezilla homepage and search for the product you want to compare.
  • Or you can go to categories to browse for your item.
  • Once that the result of your item is out, click on the comapare prices button to comapre the price of your item.

If you want to order for an item

  • After comaparing the prices for your item, the result of different prices from different stores will be displayed on your screen.
  • After comaparing the prices for your item, the result of different prices from different stores will be displayed on your screen.
  • After clicking, you’ll be redirected to the store website to buy and get your desired item.
