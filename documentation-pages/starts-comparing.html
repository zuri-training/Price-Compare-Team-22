Overview

Pricezilla is a price comparison platform that allows you to compare prices of household items across different retailers, stores and brands. Pricezilla solves the problem of helping YOU make a well-informed decision on the most cost-effective choice within your overall budget and on any specific features. You can compare prices on Pricezilla by:

  • Create a pricezilla account, if you havent already.
  • On our home page, click the categories section to browse through our product categories
  • Choose the categories of your choice and select your desired product.
  • If you have a product in mind , you can easily use the Search bar to find it.
  • Read through the selected products description, click on share icon to share on social media or send via email.
  • Have something to say about the product or price, click the comment icon to drop a comment.
  • Click on the Compare price button to visit the price compare page and choose from the different stores available.
  • When you ve made a choice, click on the purchase button to be redirected to your chosen store to make a purchase.
  • Return to the product page and leave a review.
